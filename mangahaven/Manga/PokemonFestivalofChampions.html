Pokemon: Festival of Champions

In a special cross-regional tournament held in the Kanto region, champions past and present gather to face off. Legendary Pokémon clash and personal rivalries ignite in this fan-celebration of the Pokémon world’s strongest.

This is the story of Red, Blue, and Green, three friends who decide to partake in the tournament.
