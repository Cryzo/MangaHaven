Record of Ragnarok

Every 1000 years, the gods hold a meeting to decide the fate of humanity. After much deliberation, they decide to end mankind. However, a valkyrie proposes a final battle — Ragnarok — where 13 humans will fight 13 gods to determine the fate of humanity. Epic showdowns begin as history's greatest humans clash with divine might.
